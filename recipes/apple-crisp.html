Apple Crisp Recipe

Apple Crisp: a delightful late-summer snack that's easy to make.

A favorite to many as summer turns into fall, apple crisp is a sweet, delicious snack that's easy to make to celebrate the changing of seasons.

Follow our recipe below to make your own batch. I recommend finding a local farm to go apple picking at for this recipe. It's a great way to support local businesses and get out and connect with the food you bring home.
