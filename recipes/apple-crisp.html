Apple Crisp Recipe

Apple Crisp: a delightful late-summer snack that's easy to make.

A favorite to many as summer turns into fall, apple crisp is a sweet, delicious snack that's easy to make to celebrate the changing of seasons.

Follow our recipe below to make your own batch.
I recommend finding a local farm to go apple picking at for this recipe. It's a great way to support local businesses and get out and connect with the food you bring home.

Ingredients


Filling

  • 5 cups fresh apples, sliced.
  • 1/2 cup granulated white or brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnammon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Topping

  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup old fashioned oats
  • 2/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup (1/2 stick) butter, melted
